Our Capabilities

Testing, Inspection
& Certification Services

Structured around the international TIC methodology — every service we offer is designed to meet the standards expected by global certification principals and local regulators alike.

Inspection Services

On-site, independent verification across the full trade lifecycle — from factory floor to port discharge.

  • Pre-Shipment Inspection (PSI) — quality & quantity verification before cargo departs
  • Container Loading Supervision (CLS) — real-time monitoring of packing operations
  • Warehouse & Inventory Audits — stock count verification and condition assessment
  • Factory & Social Audits — compliance review of production facilities
  • Marine & Draft Surveys — vessel stability and cargo quantification at port
  • Discharge Supervision — quantity & condition verification at receiving end

Testing & Sampling

Representative, traceable sampling and analytical coordination for commodities requiring chemical or physical characterisation.

  • Representative Sampling & Sealing — per ISO / ASTM / commodity-specific standards
  • On-site Quality Verification — visual and physical assessment of goods
  • Laboratory Coordination — liaison with accredited labs for chemical analysis
  • Commodity Grading — agricultural, mineral, and manufactured goods
  • Sample Custody Chain — tamper-evident, traceable sample management

Verification & Certification Support

Acting as the authorised local inspection body under major TIC principals, enabling global certificate issuance from local data.

  • One-off specialist inspections on principal instruction
  • Standards Compliance Verification — ISO, EAC regulatory requirements
  • Trade Finance Support — inspection reports accepted by banks & insurers

Reporting & Documentation

Accurate, audit-ready paperwork delivered fast — formatted to your principal's specifications or regulatory requirements.

  • Inspection Certificates — internationally formatted, signatory-ready
  • Survey Reports — detailed narrative and photographic records
  • Non-Conformance Reports (NCR) — for disputed or failed inspections
  • 24-Hour Turnaround — standard on all routine inspections
  • Secure digital delivery — encrypted, client-portal ready

ACAI was founded with a clear purpose: to close the operational gap between global TIC principals and the ground-level precision that African trade demands. We are local inspectors with international discipline.

Our Story

Built on the
ground, not
a boardroom.

ACAI emerged from a simple observation: the world's best testing and inspection companies need qualified, trustworthy local teams to execute their mandates in East Africa. The operational infrastructure had to follow international standards, yet be nimble enough to function at port, at the warehouse, and at the farm gate.

We are that team. Our founders came from senior TIC industry roles and built ACAI specifically to support sub-contracting mandates from global principals, while also offering direct inspection services to exporters, importers, and financiers who need independent verification.
